Format Canvas Gradebook

This Feature allows you to format exported Canvas Gradebook CSV file for uploading into Faculty Center’s Grade Roster.

Who Can use Format Canvas Gradebook?
  • Root Account Administrator
  • Sub-Account Administrator
  • Support Consultant
  • Teachers and other course managers who normally have access to the People tool
How to Use Format Canvas Gradebook?
  • Click the Format Canvas Gradebook button in Canvas Course Manager.
  • Follow the instructions from the Requirement section on the “Upload your CSV File” page.
  • Drag and drop, or click the link to upload your saved CSV file.
  • The tool will convert your file to an MPathways compatible format and download it back to your computer.
